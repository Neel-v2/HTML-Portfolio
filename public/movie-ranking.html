The Best Movies According To Me

My Top 3 Favorite Movies Of All Time

Avengers: Infinity War

This was Marvel at their best, and this movie had a very good storyline following all the characters that were built up over the past years, with an amazing plot twist ending.

The Martian

Everything in this movie could realisticly happen, and Andy Weir made sure of that when he wrote the book. It is very adventurous and has sort of an "anything could happen" feel

Spiderman: Into The Spiderverse

The animation in this movie was absolutely stunning and all the characters were well thought out and had a good place in the movie.
